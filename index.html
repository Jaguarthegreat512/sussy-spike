Gadgets:



These are gadgets, they work with a specific cooldown, and they are pretty poweful.

The first gadget works like this:

It shoots out spikes all around him 3 times, dealing damage all around him



The second gadget works like this:

It creates a plant on the ground, that can be used as cover (example 1st video) or once broken it heals players, including yourself, around it (example second video).



By the way i forgot to mention you have pick either/or gadget, you cannot have both at the same time.
